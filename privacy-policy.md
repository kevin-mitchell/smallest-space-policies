**Privacy Policy**

This privacy policy applies to the smallest.space app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Kevin Mitchell (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Free service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

**Information Collection and Use**

The Application is a local device provisioning tool. It does not collect, transmit, or store personal information on remote servers. All operations performed by the Application occur locally on your device and communicate directly with the hardware device being configured.

The Application may collect the following limited technical information for diagnostic purposes:

*   The operating system you use on your mobile device
*   Application crash reports and error logs

The Application does **not** collect your name, email address, location, browsing history, or any other personally identifiable information.

**Permissions Used by This Application**

The Application requests the following device permissions, each for a specific purpose:

*   **Camera:** The Application uses your device camera solely to scan QR codes printed on the hardware device. Camera access is required to initiate device pairing. No photos or video are captured, stored, or transmitted.

*   **Bluetooth:** The Application uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) to discover and communicate with compatible hardware devices for Wi-Fi provisioning. Bluetooth is the primary means of sending configuration data to the device. No Bluetooth data is shared with third parties.

*   **Location (Android only):** Android requires location permission to scan for Bluetooth Low Energy devices. The Application does **not** collect, store, use, or transmit your location. This permission is requested solely to satisfy the Android operating system requirement for BLE scanning. On Android 12 and above, the Application uses the `BLUETOOTH_SCAN` permission with the `neverForLocation` flag to make clear that location is not inferred from Bluetooth scans.

*   **Wi-Fi and Network State:** The Application requests Wi-Fi and network state permissions to deliver Wi-Fi credentials to the device being configured and to verify network connectivity during the provisioning process. No network data is collected or stored.

*   **Vibration:** The Application may use device vibration to provide haptic feedback during the provisioning workflow.

**Third Party Access**

The Application does not transmit your data to third parties. The Application does not use advertising SDKs, analytics platforms, or data brokers.
